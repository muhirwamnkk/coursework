Services Offered

Counselling
We host weekly counselling sessions for free on different mental health issues that the youth are faceing
Our counselling sessions provide a safe, confidential space where individuals can openly share their struggles and receive professional guidance. Whether you’re dealing with stress, anxiety, depression, or life transitions, our trained counsellors help you find healing, clarity, and strength one step at a time.


Support group meetings
Small group meetings focuesd to help those struggling with things like addictiona and depression
You don’t have to go through it alone. Our support groups bring together people with shared experiences to talk, listen, and heal together. In these small, caring communities, members find understanding, encouragement, and hope while learning from each other’s journeys.


School mental health outreachs
We reach out to different schools to spread awareness about mental health
We believe mental health awareness starts with connection. Through our outreaches, we engage schools, youth centers, and local communities to educate, inspire, and break the stigma surrounding mental health. Together, we’re creating open spaces for conversation, understanding, and lasting impact.
